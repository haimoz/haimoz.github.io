I am Haimo Zhang, a researcher in human-computer interaction. I am interested in using technologies to empower people in their daily life experiences.

  • How to feel the physical and virtual world in ways beyond our biological limits?
  • How to include ordinary passive objects into digital interactive experiences?
  • How to train our brain to be more effective in certain tasks?

In addressing these challenges, I mostly rely on intuition to conceptualize interaction experiences and use cases, while adapting various technologies and scientific methods to prototype and test my intuitions.

In my research, I have learned to work with many technologies, such as stereoscopic 3D displays, VR, information visualization toolkits, motion and touch sensors, and haptic devices. I look forward to a lifetime of learning.
