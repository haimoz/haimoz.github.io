I am a researcher in human-computer interaction. I am interested in using technologies to empower people in their daily life experiences. Specifically, I look at the interface between a human and the external environment, and aim to enhance three areas of human capabilities:

  • Perception — how we feel the external environment.
  • Cognition — how we understand the external environment.
  • Action — how we affect the external environment.

In my research, I have learned to work with many technologies, such as stereoscopic 3D displays, VR, information visualization toolkits, motion and touch sensors, and haptic devices. I look forward to a lifetime of learning.
