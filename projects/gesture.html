GestKeyboard

gestural interaction on physical keyboards

Keyboard allows for efficient text entry, and (but!) little more than that. We still need mouse and hot keys for efficient operation in front of a computer. Can we introduce gestural interaction to physical keyboards, to get more stuff done?

In the GestKeyboard project, an uninstrumented ordinary physical keyboard is treated as a low-resolution touchpad, since the key press and key release events approximate the trajectory of finger movement while it swipes across the keyboard, activating the keys along the way.
